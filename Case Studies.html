Developing our Protopersona: Maya Thompson

We decided to take the persona angle of the donator for our project due to time constraints. Other personas we would have also taken would have been those of the services-seeking persona.
User Survey and Interviews
Developed/Implemented:

Created a 8 questionnaire survey:  5 multiple choice and 3 open ended questions.
(12 user participants)

Created an interview plan with 8 question semi-structured involving before and after visiting the organizations page

User Empathy
Now that we have seen what the users have seen and explained from their experience from the Inclusion Center’s site we can now refine and properly define our user persona. From there we can use Maya Thompson as our guide to indentifying the root problems and designing solutions to meet our users needs.
