Project Overview
Background and Motivation

As a nonprofit and charitable organization, the Inclusion Center for Community and Justice rely heavily on donations,charitable contributions, and volunteerism to maintain their mission.

Their Mission Statement:

As a human relations organization dedicated to overcoming hatred, bias and exclusionary practices, we build inclusive communities through experiential learning, intentional dialogue, fostering relationships, and the cultivation of tomorrow's leaders. We strive to support our community through engagement, respect, concern, empathy and understanding.

My team and I chose this organization because they provide great services within the local community and provide workshops, retreats, and events that attract all members of the community to join and share experiences.
